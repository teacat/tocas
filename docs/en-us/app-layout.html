Introduction

This is an application-specific component.

It is usually placed at the top level of the application and is divided into side, top, bottom and main content blocks before placing the important content in them.

App Sidebar and App Navbar are often used together along with this component.

Fluid

One cell can fill up the remaining space and squeeze the rest of the cells to the minimum width. When using this style, it is better to have a fixed width for the other cells to avoid errors in the extended style.
